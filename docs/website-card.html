Stop Codon Finder

Scan any genome for stop codons across all six reading frames, map each one to its gene, and export to GFF3, BED, FASTA, CSV/JSON, or publication figures — free, in the browser, with nothing uploaded. Supports FASTA, GenBank and GFF3, the full set of NCBI genetic codes, and circular genomes.

  • Six-frame scan
  • Gene annotation
  • 23 genetic codes
  • GFF3 / BED / FASTA export
  • 100% client-side
